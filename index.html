• RAILEYE

An app designed to give you realtime updates about your transit journey.

Why Raileye

The ability to report issues as they occur allows other users to benefit from the fastest route possible. Not only does it benefit others, and subsequently help you, Raileye has a rewards system. The more you report accurate information, the more discounts on your transportation you will recieve.

Future

As public transportation is growing in Paris, so will raileye. With Raileye you can keep up to date with the Grand Paris Express construction as well as how to use it best to your advantage.
